Merge 3 Mode Strategy: Staging, Timing, and Space Control

Triplet Mechanics

Three identical tiles merge into one higher tile. Growth is slower; careless staging quickly fills the board.

Staging Pairs

Keep two identical tiles staged near the pipeline and spawn the third on demand. This avoids blocking lanes and preserves flexibility.

Space-First Play

  • Maintain two empty cells at all times for alignment slack.
  • Clear stray singles before creating new pairs.
  • Use two-direction bias to keep ordering intact.
Drill: Spend a run creating only controlled triplets—no opportunistic merges. Train patience and timing.
