How to Play 2468 & 2048

2468 and 2048 are fun and addictive number puzzle games. Here's how to play:

  • Use your arrow keys (or swipe on mobile) to move the tiles.
  • When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!
  • Try to reach the 2468 or 2048 tile to win.
  • Plan your moves and keep your highest tile in a corner for best results.
  • Practice patience and strategy to achieve higher scores.
